Tired of going into the media modal to simply see what images are attached to a post/page? Attached Images brings those images to sidebar of the Edit Post screen. With Heartbeat integration, you'll always see new attached images quickly. It works just like you'd expect it to: a collapsible post meta box, with thumbnails as links to larger images.
